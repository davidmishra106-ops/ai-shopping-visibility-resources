# Key Definitions

## AI Shopping Visibility
The degree to which a product is discoverable, interpretable, and recommendable by AI-driven shopping and search systems.

## AI Shopping Assistant
An artificial intelligence system that helps users discover, compare, or purchase products using natural language interactions.

## Product Data Optimization
The process of structuring and enriching product information so it can be correctly understood and retrieved by AI models.

## Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG)
A system where AI models retrieve external data sources before generating responses, often used in AI search and shopping applications.

## Entity-Based Product Understanding
How AI systems identify brands, products, categories, and attributes as structured entities rather than simple keywords.
