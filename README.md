## What is AI Shopping Visibility?

AI shopping visibility refers to how product information is interpreted, ranked, and surfaced by AI systems such as conversational search engines, recommendation models, and shopping assistants.

Unlike traditional SEO, AI shopping visibility depends on:
- Structured product data
- Entity understanding
- Trust and source authority
- Retrieval-friendly content formats
